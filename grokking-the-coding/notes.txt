Grokking the Coding Interview: Patterns for Coding Questions
Prereqs: You need to have a basic understanding of common data-structures like Array, LinkedList, HashMap, Stack, Queue, Heap, and Graph. You should also be familiar with Recursion and Big-O.

16 Patterns
Overall, the course has around 150 problems mapped to 16 patterns.

We will make use of Breadth-First Search and Depth-First Search to solve problems related to Trees and Graphs. 
Similarly, we will also cover Dynamic Programming, Backtracking, Recursion, Greedy algorithms, and Divide & Conquer.
